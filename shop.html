Store & supporter FAQ

How quickly are ranks applied?

Ranks are delivered instantly once your payment is confirmed. If you run into any issues, open a ticket in Discord and our staff will help right away.

Which payment methods do you support?

We partner with Stripe to support major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and a growing list of regional payment methods.

Can I gift a rank to someone else?

Absolutely! Enter their in-game username during checkout and they'll receive the perks automatically.

Do purchases expire?

Permanent ranks never expire. Seasonal bundles remain active for the length of the season and include renewal reminders before they conclude.
